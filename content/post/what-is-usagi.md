title: What is Usagi
---

**Usagi** (Japanese: うさぎ, literally "rabbit") is one of the core characters in *Chiikawa* (ちいかわ), the manga and anime series by Nagano. On the series wiki they are described as a small yellow rabbit who appears alongside Chiikawa, Hachiware, and the rest of the cast.

## Personality

Usagi is energetic and mischievous, often teasing Chiikawa and stirring up trouble in a playful way. They rarely speak in normal sentences; instead they use short, expressive sounds and reactions that fans recognize from the show. Despite the chaotic vibe, the character is often portrayed as surprisingly capable and decisive when something important happens—calm or unfazed in situations that stress other characters.

## Appearance

Usagi is drawn as a round, yellow rabbit with long ears, simple dot eyes, and the soft pink blush marks typical of *Chiikawa* designs. The look is minimal and cute, consistent with the series’ overall art style.
